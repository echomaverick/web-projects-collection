Stack Bounce

Description

Get ready for addictive fun with Stack Bounce! In this game, your objective is to stack blocks as high as you can while bouncing a ball to keep the stack growing. Each block you successfully stack adds to your score, but be careful - if you miss and drop a block, the game ends! With its simple yet challenging gameplay, Stack Bounce will keep you entertained for hours as you strive for the highest score.

Controls:

Click or tap anywhere on the screen to release the ball and make it bounce. Aim the ball to land it on top of the stack of blocks to continue building. Timing is key - release the ball at the right moment to ensure a successful bounce.

Tips and tricks:

  • Focus on timing your bounces to land the ball precisely on the center of each block.
  • As the stack grows taller, the speed of the ball increases, so stay alert!
  • Collect power-ups and bonuses to help you build higher stacks and achieve higher scores.
